Ultimate: The Sport

What Is Ultimate?

Ultimate is a flying disc sport played all over the world. Originally known as "Ultimate Frisbee," it is regarded by some players as the fastest-growing sport in the world.

Who Can Play?

The beauty of Ultimate is its accessibility. Anyone can play Ultimate, regardless of experience, skill level, or gender. Competitive Ultimate is typically played in three divisions:

  • Open (Men's)
  • Women's
  • Mixed
